How we roast

Charge to drop, in four stages.

0–4 min

Drying

Green beans lose surface moisture as drum temperature climbs steadily toward 300°F.

4–8 min

Maillard

Sugars and amino acids react, building the base flavor and turning beans from green to tan.

8–9.5 min

First crack

Beans audibly pop as internal steam pressure releases; this is where roast level starts to diverge.

9.5–11 min

Development & drop

We hold or extend this stage to hit light, medium, or dark, then drop beans into the cooling tray.
